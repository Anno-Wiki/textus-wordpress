What is TEXTUS?

In a nutshell it is an open source platform for working with collections of texts. It harnesses the power of semantic web technologies and delivers them in a simple and intuitive interface so that students, researchers and teachers can share and collaborate around collections of texts. TEXTUS is a project of the Open Knowledge Foundation. The home page for the TEXTUS project is at textusproject.org.

What is the state of this beta version?

This instance of TEXTUS allows you to upload texts in WikiText or plain text format, and to read these texts and create annotations.

Click on the 'All Texts' link to see texts which have been uploaded, click on any text in the list to start reading and see annotations from other users.

Optionally, either log in or register an account using the panel in the top right of the screen. Please do not use any valuable passwords! there's no guarantee of security for this particular instance of the server.

Once logged in you can create annotations by selecting text in the reader interface and entering your comment into the editor which will appear. Click anywhere else on the text to dismiss the editor without saving your annotations.

At the moment all annotations are public, and there is no way to delete them. Play nice.
